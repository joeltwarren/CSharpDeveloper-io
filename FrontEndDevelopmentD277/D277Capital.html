History of Georgia Capitals

  • Savannah:


    The first capital, serving as the center for the colony's government in 1733.

  • Augusta:


    The second capital, serving from 1785 to 1795.

  • Louisville:


    The thrid capital, serving in 1796.

  • Milledgeville:


    The fourth capital, serving from 1804 to 1868.

  • Atlanta:


    The fifth and current capital, serving since 1868.
