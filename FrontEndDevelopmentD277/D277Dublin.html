Interesting Facts - Dublin Georgia

  • Irish roots:


    Dublin was founded on December 9th in 1812 by Jonathan Sawyer, an Irish immigrant who agreed to donate land for public buildings in exchange for naming the town after his wife's ancestral home in Dublin, Ireland.

  • Annual Peanut Festival:


    A festival held each October, featuring parades, music, and plenty of peanut treats.

  • Activities:


    Venues at Dublin Carnegie and Theatre Dublin offer local artwork, live music, and theatrical performances. Dublin Greenway and the local Oconee River provide hiking, biking, and fishing.
